The Method

The Small Moments Method is a gentle, four-week 1:1 journey for women who are quietly waiting to feel enough. Together we lift your worth off everything you've tied it to — how you look, how much you earn, how well you show up for everyone else — and return it to where it has always belonged: within you. Your worth was never out there to be earned or proven; it's already yours. The work is simply learning to feel it, in the small, ordinary moments of everyday life, until being enough becomes simply who you are.

Four gentle movements

01

Notice

Begin to catch the small moments you usually rush past.

02

Name

Put words to what you feel, and to the worth that was always there.

03

Anchor

Let that sense of worth settle into your body, so it becomes familiar.

04

Return

Build a daily ritual that brings you home to yourself, again and again.
